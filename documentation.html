Zoomie App Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to the documentation of Zoomie App. This guide will help users understand how to use Zoomie app to access Zoom meetings and recordings on their Android TV.

Getting Started

To get started with your app, follow these steps:

  1. Download and install the app from app store on your Android TV device.
  2. Launch the app and log in using your Zoom credentials.
  3. Once logged in, you can access your Zoom meetings and recordings.

Features

Your app offers the following features:

  • Access and view Zoom meeting recordings on your Android TV.
  • Play Recordings directly in the app with a powerful video player.
